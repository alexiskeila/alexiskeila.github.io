Alexis Viera's Portfolio

LS/Dysmorphia (work in progress)

This is my current project, a digital comic book that appears to chronicle a trip, but is later revealed to be describing the mind-altering effects of body dysmorphia.

Ads Annual, Edition 2016

Ads Annual is a look into the past of sexist advertising hosted on a digital platform. At first, it is just a compilation, but a few begin to come to life.

Deported

"Deported" is a look into a conversation between two sisters who faced a terrible decision, and the strain their choices caused in their relationship.

Mother Darling (excerpt)

"Mother Darling" is a short story written in a modernized Virginia Woolf style that tests the relationship between reader and narrator.

Second Wave (excerpt)

"Second Wave" is a short story set in the 1950s about a housewife who has discovered feminism and dying to take action.
